Get started with our codelabs

During the summit, Chrome engineers will be on site and on the Slack channel to answer all your questions as you build your very first set of progressive web apps by following one of our codelabs at g.co/codelabs/pwadevsummit.

Day 1: A deep dive into Progressive Web Apps

Learn how to build great web experiences that load quickly and are amazing to use in every browser and then how to weave in the latest platform technologies to deliver a great Progressive Web App.

Day 2: Continue the deep dive with more sessions and codelabs

We'll continue sessions on Day 2, then break for Codelabs and Office Hours until the end of the day. Even if you're not in attendance, the code labs will be available online for you to work from the comfort of your own home.

Shuttle & parking information

We will have an event shuttle running between Victoria Hotel (across from Amsterdam’s Centraal Station) and Theater Amsterdam running every 30 minutes on 20 June from 7:30-10:30AM, and from Theater Amsterdam to the Victoria Hotel from 20:30-22:30. The shuttle will also run on 21 June from 7:30-9:30AM from the Victoria Hotel (across from Amsterdam’s Centraal Station) and 16:00-18:00 every 30 minutes from Theater Amsterdam to the Victoria Hotel. Please look for the Progressive Web App Dev Summit 2016 sign.

There is limited parking at Theater Amsterdam. We strongly recommend you take the shuttle, public transportation, or a taxi.
